About Us

Brian & Kitty Jones — Cougar Hunt

I’m Brian Jones… We were surprised a cougar hunt was available the week after our wedding. Extreme Honeymoons handled all the details and even gave pre-trip archery instruction.

Our leader, Tom Henry, ran a team of hounds. The light snow made tracking ideal. We saw three cougars but didn’t get a clean shot. It was a beautiful, exhausting weekend. Thanks for the memories!

David & Mary Farnsworth — Heliskiing on Kings Peak

We wanted a rip-roaring honeymoon. With expert pilots and guides, we skied Utah’s tallest peak after a big dump of fresh snow. Six runs a day—each one an adventure. Gear double-checked, no issues.

We’ll tell all our friends—great way to spend a honeymoon. Thanks for handling the details so we could enjoy it together.

Peter & Polly Renquist — Moab Mountain Biking

We wanted an unforgettable trip. A cozy B&B, early breakfasts, shuttle to the trailhead, GPS + maps, plenty of water, and miles of slickrock. Couldn’t have done it without your help.

We still smile every time we look at the photos.
